Baseball Simulation

This project simulates baseball games between two fictional teams. Every pitch is simulated, and a new game is played each time you reload the page.

Features Include:

  • Every pitch is simulated through a complex series of probabilities
  • A wide variety of stats and information, all organized in a clean user interface
  • Individualized player stats that influence their performance
